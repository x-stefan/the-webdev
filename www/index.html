About me

I‘m Stefan Stöhr — a Web Developer based in Aschaffenburg, Bavaria Germany. I like developing meaningful concepts, coding bespoke websites and learning new things along the way.

Between 2006 and 2010 I completed a Bachelor degree in Computer Science at the Hochschule Darmstadt - University of Applied Science and I'm currently working for the advertising company B2 Communications GmbH.

I’ve been developing websites for over 6 years, going from building small, static microsites up to big CMS driven company websites. Beside that I also have experience in managing projects, leading a team and researching/introducing new technologies.

Technology Stack

Beside HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and PHP I'm also using Sass and gulp.js to improve and speed up my development workflow. I'm especially using BrowserSync together with gulp.js for the nasty reloading on changes. Git is my weapon of choice for the battle against data loss and chaos.

I'm also experienced with different database architectures, but but I'm mainly using MySQL in the last years.

I prefer MacOS as OS, but that doesn't mean I can't work with Windows. And being familiar with Linux Servers (Debian, Ubuntu and SuSe Linux) is just a part of the game.
